How to Do the Dictation

  1. Listen until the audio stops. Then write. Don't write while listening.
  2. Hard time? Do 3 sentences. Increase next lesson.
  3. Feel free to repeat sentences, just don't obsess.
  4. Can't understand a word? It's fine. Just continue.
  5. Work 10–15 minutes max per session. Then stop.
