Enable English Subtitles (Auto-Translate)

5 quick slides. Use arrows, dots, or keyboard (← →).

Step 1 of 5

Open subtitle settings: Tap the gear (⚙️) or CC icon at the bottom of the player.

Step 2 of 5

Open the subtitles list: Tap Untertitel to view available tracks.

Step 3 of 5

Select the provided track: Choose the available subtitles (e.g., Deutsch).

Step 4 of 5

Tap Automatisch übersetzen: This enables translation options.

Step 5 of 5

Pick Englisch: Close the menu — subtitles will now display in English.
